A Journey to Fitness is one of the most challenging endeavors

The challenge arises various reasons

Here are some of the Challenges include:

  • Starting: It takes profound will to start on the Journey
  • Showing up consequtively: It is usually easy to quit in the early stages
  • Understanding your body: It requires more than just working out. Both diet and exercise go hand-in-hand.
    1. One might not lose any weight despite exercising if not following a diet plan
    2. A bad diet might lead to injuries

The key things to full fitness and weight loss are governed by three major aspects

The Aspects are:

  1. Consistency
  2. Dieting and hydration
  3. Listening to your body - When tired, rest.
